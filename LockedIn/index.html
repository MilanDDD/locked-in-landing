Core Features

Built Around
How You Actually Live.

Four categories. One combined score. Every part of your day counts toward something.

🎯
Daily Missions

A fresh set of objectives every day across fitness, nutrition, and discipline. Clear targets that give structure to your day and reward you for following through.

→ Resets each day at midnight
⚡
XP & Streaks

Every completed action earns XP that feeds your daily score and your league standing. Streaks track how consistently you're showing up — the longer they run, the more they reflect.

→ Consistency builds momentum
🏆
Weekly Leagues

You're placed in a league of players at your level. Top performers promote. The bottom get relegated. It resets every week, so there's always something worth playing for.

→ Bronze through Diamond tiers
💪
Fitness & Fuel

→ XP earned from real training data
How It Works

Simple System.
Real Results.

The mechanics are straightforward. What makes it work is that everything connects.

01
Log Your Daily Actions

Track your workout, meals, and progress each day. Every action you take maps to a mission. Your day has a score — and you can see it build in real time.

02
Earn XP. Build Your Streak.

Completed missions earn XP that goes straight to your league score. Your streak tracks how many days you've stayed consistent — it's a record worth maintaining.

03
Climb Your Weekly League

Your XP determines your position in a weekly league. Finish in the top spots and you promote. The league resets each week, giving you a clean window to push harder.

The Difference

More Than
A Habit Tracker.

Most apps track what you do. Locked In connects your actions to something that actually keeps you coming back.

🎯
Built Around Identity

The goal isn't just to log activity — it's to build a picture of who you're becoming. Your score, streak, and rank reflect how seriously you're taking your own progress.

⚔️
Competition That Means Something

Weekly leagues with real promotion and relegation add a layer of context that personal records can't. You're not just tracking yourself — you're measuring yourself against others doing the same work.

🔗
Everything in One Place

Fitness, nutrition, and discipline feed into the same score. When everything is connected, it's easier to see the full picture — and harder to ignore the gaps.

Locked In vs. Everything Else

  • ✕ No competition — you're only racing yourself
  • ✕ Streaks with no real consequence for breaking them
  • ✕ No score that reflects your full day
  • ✕ Easy to ignore when motivation dips

  • ✓ Every rep, meal, and mission earns XP toward one score
  • ✓ Weekly league of 18 players — top performers promote
  • ✓ Streak reflects how many consecutive days you've hit your targets
  • ✓ Protein goals and calorie limits tied directly to daily missions
  • ✓ League resets weekly — there's always something to push for
